    • to address any enquires you may have raised via the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website; and/or

  • Your personal information may be shared with any Abdul Latif Jameel business. Your personal information may then be held by such an Abdul Latif Jameel business(es) as part of its enquiry / customer database.
  • Your personal information may be shared with selected third parties including:
    • Marketing services providers to deliver e-newsletter and updates.
    • Market research businesses or similar organisations for the undertaking and analysis of customer surveys and feedback.
    • Business partners, suppliers and sub-contractors for the performance of any contract.
    • Analytics and search engine providers who assist in the improvement and optimisation of the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website.
  • Your personal information may be disclosed to third parties:
    • In the event any business of Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt sells or buys any business or assets, in which case your personal data may be disclosed to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets.
    • If an Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt business or substantially all of its assets are acquired by a third party, in which case personal data held by it about its customers will be one of the transferred assets.
    • If there is a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website’s Terms of Use (hyperlink) and other agreements; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of any Abdul Latif Jameel business, customers, or others. This includes without limitation exchanging information with other companies and organisations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

Where Your Personal Data is Stored

The Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website will be managed and operated in Egypt. The data collected from you via the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website will be stored on servers currently located in Egypt. By providing your personal data to the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website, you agree to this storing or processing.The Company will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure your data is treated securely, to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure and in accordance with this privacy policy. Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although all steps reasonably necessary will be taken to protect your personal data, the security of your data transmitted to the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website cannot be guaranteed; any such transmission is at your own risk. Information submitted via the Abdul Latif Jameel Finance Egypt Website is normally unprotected until it reaches the Company. Users are requested not to send confidential details or credit card numbers, for example, by email or the enquiry form. Once your information is received, strict procedures and security features will be used to try to prevent unauthorised access.



For How Long is Your Personal Information  Kept?

The time period for which your information is kept varies according to what your information is used for. In some cases there are legal or regulatory requirements to keep data for a minimum period. Unless there is a specific legal requirement to keep the information, it will be retained for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the data was collected or for which it is to be further processed. At any time you can ask for your stored information to be deleted from the Company’s database using the contact details below. However, some of your information may be retained in some circumstances, such as to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, enforce terms and conditions or as required by regulators. Further, in some circumstances such information is never entirely removed from databases due to technical constraints and systems back-ups.
